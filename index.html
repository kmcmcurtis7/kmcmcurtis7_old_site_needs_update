ABOUT

With 14+ years of corporate America, I decided to find something I enjoyed and use skills in which I excel. I enjoy solving problems, coding, training others and enpowering those considering technology as a career.

I enjoy the detail oriented skills needed for coding and see coding as a way to improve and expand my skillsets. I believe everyone should have a basic understanding of how websites are created whether they plan on learning to code as a career or not.

My current business skills encompass salesforce.com, MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, Access, Lync), Crystal Reports, Adobe Writer, Enhanced Cliq (Citrix), CCS (AS400 mainframe), Workstation, NMWB, Umpire , Lotus Notes, Midway, Clipper and Compass.

My current technical skills encompass Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3, Twitter Bootstrap, JSON, Postgres, APIs (AJAX, Google), jQuery, Javascript. Tools used include: Sublime Text, Brackets, Google Dev Tools, Slack and many others.

I am currently a teacher assistant for Tech Talent South and Girl Develop It - RDU. In my spare time, I am also working on my Front-End Developer certificate via freecodecamp.com.
